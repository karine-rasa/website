About
the writer

Hello, my name is Karine. I studied audiovisual and digital communication, and I currently work as a freelance ghostwriter based in Madagascar. But you can already see that in my resume or on LinkedIn. I am guessing that you’ve clicked on this page because you want to know a little more about me as a writer.

Here’s what you should know: I’ve always been fascinated by art in general. But fictional literature holds a special place in my heart — ever since I finished reading my first book at the age of eight. There’s something almost magical about getting so attached to things that aren’t even real — characters, worlds, adventures. I find it extraordinary how literature immerses us in a whole new universe, perfectly harmonizing the author’s imagery and the reader’s imagination. Reading offers a unique kind of escape — it’s like traveling for days without ever leaving the couch. And even when the journey ends, that bittersweet feeling lingers. The satisfaction of turning the last page is often mixed with the sting of an inevitable goodbye. Characters get under your skin… Events deeply mark you… sometimes, for a lifetime. That is the feeling I want to bring to others.

But as I started writing my own stories, it taught me that books aren’t just an experience for the reader, but the writer as well. That’s when I realized how deep this art really is. It’s not just a means of expression, a collection of events, or a tool for messaging and propaganda. Writing is putting a piece of yourself on paper. It’s delicate, it’s raw, it’s pure, it’s precious. It’s not something to take lightly. In a way, it is both freeing and intimate. But with intimacy can come isolation. Which is probably why I haven't been able to go through most of my own manuscripts and scripts… Then, after collaborating with other people, I discovered the advantages of teamwork on a creative project. Not only does it make the writing process more fun and exciting, but it also enriches the result. It doesn't take away the personal aspect of a piece. If anything else, it strengthens it. So I fell in love with helping others bring their stories to life. Talking together, bouncing ideas off each other, merging our research and perspectives — that only polishes the edges of this raw diamond of a story.

That’s why I decided to offer my services. Writing doesn’t have to be a solitary job. I want to help people share their vision, one that resonates with both the author and the reader/spectator. It’s my mission to make sure your voice and your heart are presented in the best possible way.

Karine Rasa
